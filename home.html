Adopt A Senior

A way to connect with those around you



Covid-19 has impacted people of all nationalities, demographics, and ages making it difficult to remain together. Adopt A Senior gives the unique opportunity for young adults and adults alike to reach out to one outside their generation and create a bond that will go beyond the pandemic.
